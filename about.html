About Me

I am a web developer focused on building modern, professional websites with clean UI, accessible experiences, and readable code.

Story Approach Experience

Story

I'm a self-taught developer whose curiosity for technology turned into a passion for creating. As a Building Technology student, I've always loved sketching ideas, bringing them to life, and adding the right details, and I found the same joy in coding, where lines of code transform into interactive and beautiful digital experiences. I focus on frontend visuals, interactivity, and problem-solving, with a friendly yet professional approach. My goal is simple; to build reliable, modern websites that help businesses grow and leave clients happy with the results.

I create websites that are as functional as they are visually engaging, focusing on clean design, smooth interactivity, and attention to detail. By staying up to date with modern techniques, I ensure every project not only looks polished but helps clients grow and succeed.

Philosophy & Approach

Every project is different, but my process stays consistent: keep the user in mind, design with clarity, and build with care. Here's how I usually work:

  • Discovery: Understand client goals and project constraints.
  • Design: Create wireframes and UI with clarity and usability in mind.
  • Build: Write clean, modular HTML/CSS/JS with progressive enhancement.
  • Deliver: Run performance and accessibility checks before launch.
